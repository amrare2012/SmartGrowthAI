The Problem

SMEs often face high training costs that lead to under-skilled employees and poor management practices. Additionally, many SME owners lack formal education, limiting their ability to implement innovative strategies.

High Training Costs

Due to budget constraints, SMEs often avoid investing in employee training, resulting in low skill levels and inefficient management.

Educational Gaps Among SME Owners

Only 40% of Latino SME owners in the U.S. have a bachelor's degree, which hampers strategic decision-making and innovation.

Our Solution

SmartGrowth AI offers a fully automated, cost-effective training platform that leverages AI to assess skills, generate personalized learning paths, and drive continuous employee development.

Solution Overview

Our platform delivers customized training modules and flexible, self-paced courses, tailored to the needs of various industries.

AI Skill Assessments

Employees undergo an initial AI-based evaluation to identify skill gaps, enabling the system to craft targeted training plans.

Flexible, Online Training

The training is delivered entirely online, allowing employees to learn at their own pace without the need for physical training spaces.

Incentives & Rewards

Participants earn official certificates, bonuses, and leadership opportunities while receiving public recognition for their achievements.

Accountability Measures

Our system mandates a minimum of two training sessions per week, with automated reminders and a gradual penalization process to ensure consistent participation.

Performance Reports

Managers receive real-time, AI-generated reports detailing employee progress and areas for improvement, enabling data-driven decisions.
