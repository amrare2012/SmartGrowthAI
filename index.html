Revolutionizing SME training with AI-powered, cost-effective, and scalable learning solutions.

The Problem

SMEs often struggle with high training costs, leading to under-skilled employees and poor business management practices. Additionally, many SME owners lack formal education, limiting their ability to implement innovative strategies and manage their businesses effectively.

High Training Costs

Due to budget constraints, many SMEs avoid investing in employee training, which results in low skill levels and inefficient management.

Educational Gaps Among SME Owners

Only 40% of Latino SME owners in the U.S. hold a bachelor's degree, hindering strategic decision-making and innovation.

Our Solution

SmartGrowth AI provides a fully automated, cost-effective online training platform that leverages AI to assess skills, create personalized learning paths, and ensure continuous employee development.

Solution Overview

Our platform delivers customized training modules, flexible learning schedules, and AI-powered assessments to drive efficiency and innovation in SMEs.

AI Skill Assessments

Employees undergo an initial AI-based evaluation to identify skill gaps, allowing the system to generate tailored training plans focused on improvement areas.

Flexible, Online Training

Our self-paced courses are accessible entirely online, eliminating the need for physical training spaces and additional trainers.

Incentives & Rewards

Employees earn official certificates, bonuses, and leadership opportunities. Our system features attractive rewards and public recognition to motivate continuous learning.

Accountability Measures

Employees are required to complete at least two training sessions per week. AI-generated reminders and a gradual penalization system ensure consistent participation.

Performance Reports

Managers receive real-time, AI-driven reports on employee progress, enabling data-driven decisions for talent development.
